## Parts of the house

```
Livingroom -> Sala
Diningroom -> Comedor
Kitchen -> Cocina
Bedroom -> Habitación, Dormitorio
Bathroom -> Baño
Restroom -> Baño público
Attic -> Ático
Basement -> Sótano
Backyard -> Patio trasero
Yard -> Patio
Garden -> Jardin
Garage -> Garaje
```
